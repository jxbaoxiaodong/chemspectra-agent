## Style Prompt
Scientific instrument interface meets modern data visualization. Inspired by laboratory FTIR equipment displays — amber readouts on deep navy backgrounds. Clean, precise, authoritative. Not flashy — trustworthy. The aesthetic says "this is a real tool built by a real scientist, not a weekend hackathon project."

## Colors
- Background: #0C1222 (deep space navy)
- Primary Accent: #F59E0B (instrument amber/gold)
- Secondary: #38BDF8 (sky blue — supporting, links, diagrams)
- Text Primary: #E2E8F0 (warm off-white)
- Text Muted: #94A3B8 (slate, secondary text)
- Surface: #1E293B (card backgrounds, elevated panels)
- Surface Border: #334155 (subtle borders)
- Success/Tool: #22C55E (tool active badges)
- Alert: #EF4444 (attention points)

## Typography
- Headlines: "Inter", sans-serif (700-800 weight)
- Body: "Inter", sans-serif (400 weight)
- Code/Data: "JetBrains Mono", monospace

## Motion
- Energy: Medium (corporate/explainer)
- Primary transition: Blur crossfade (0.5s, power2.inOut)
- Closing transition: Color dip to black (0.6s)
- Entrance easing: power3.out (crisp arrival)
- Stagger between elements: 0.12-0.18s

## What NOT to Do
- No neon glow or excessive bloom effects
- No gradient text (background-clip: text)
- No pure #000 or #fff — always tinted toward palette
- No decoration-for-decoration — every element serves narrative
- No same entrance pattern repeated within a scene
